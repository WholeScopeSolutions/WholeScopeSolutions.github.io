University Event Organizer

A standalone centralized platform to showcase events occuring around the University of Victoria† campus, providing an easy way for clubs and students to advertise and find events.

UVic has a diverse community and supports many student activities and clubs on campus. There is currently no easy way to inform students about these activities and events. This projects goal is to change that, by creating an event organization system as detailed in Teck4's Request for Proposal.
†Teck4 is not a real company. Teck4 is not affliated with the University of Victoria in any way.

*Whole Scope Solutions is not a real company. This website and all affliated content is part of an academic assignment for a class (SENG 321) at the University of Victoria.
